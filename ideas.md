## Idea 1: "Dark Forest Atelier" — Cinematic Editorial

**Design Movement**: Dark Academia meets Boutique Agency — editorial luxury with Romanian folklore undertones

**Core Principles**:
1. Cinematic darkness — deep forest greens and charcoal as base, warm amber as accent
2. Handcrafted imperfection — script fonts, organic shapes, no perfect symmetry
3. Storytelling hierarchy — every section tells a chapter, not just displays info
4. Tactile depth — grain textures, subtle vignettes, layered photography

**Color Philosophy**:
- Background: `#0d1117` (near-black with green undertone)
- Forest Deep: `#1a2318` (dark forest green)
- Amber Glow: `#c8842a` (warm cabin light)
- Cream Text: `#f5ede0` (warm ivory)
- Rose Accent: `#d4748a` (matching her Instagram pink script)
- Muted Sage: `#6b7c5e` (secondary text)

**Layout Paradigm**: Full-bleed cinematic sections with asymmetric text placement. Hero takes 100vh with parallax. Service cards use editorial magazine-style layout (not equal grid). Testimonials in a horizontal scroll strip.

**Signature Elements**:
1. Thin horizontal rule lines with small diamond/leaf dividers between sections
2. Handwritten-style section labels (e.g., "Capitolul I", "Capitolul II")
3. Vignette overlay on all hero/background images

**Interaction Philosophy**: Slow, deliberate reveals — elements fade in as you scroll, like turning pages. Hover states on cards lift with warm shadow. CTA buttons have a subtle shimmer.

**Animation**:
- Hero text: staggered fade-up on load (0.3s delay between lines)
- Section entries: fade-in with slight upward translate on scroll
- Card hover: translateY(-4px) + warm box-shadow glow
- Navigation: smooth underline draw on hover

**Typography System**:
- Display: `Playfair Display` (serif, elegant, high contrast) — headings
- Script accent: `Dancing Script` (cursive, for section labels and decorative text)
- Body: `Lato` (clean, readable, slightly warm) — paragraphs
- Hierarchy: 72px hero → 42px section → 24px card → 16px body

## Idea 2: "The Transylvania Edition" — Rustic Luxury Magazine

**Design Movement**: Luxury travel magazine meets artisan brand — think Kinfolk meets Monocle, Romanian edition

**Core Principles**:
1. Editorial whitespace — generous breathing room, content floats in space
2. Warm earth tones — terracotta, warm beige, forest green
3. Asymmetric photo grids — magazine-style collages, not uniform cards
4. Serif authority — commanding display typography that owns the page

**Color Philosophy**:
- Background: `#f7f0e6` (warm parchment)
- Terracotta: `#b85c38` (earthy, warm, Romanian)
- Forest: `#2d4a2d` (deep pine)
- Charcoal: `#2a2a2a` (text)
- Gold: `#c9a84c` (accent)

**Layout Paradigm**: Light background with dark photography inserts. Alternating left/right text-image sections. Horizontal scrolling gallery strip. Sticky navigation with transparent-to-solid scroll behavior.

**Signature Elements**:
1. Thin serif rule lines with small ornamental dots
2. Pull-quote typography in large italic serif
3. Photo collage grids with slight rotation

**Interaction Philosophy**: Elegant and restrained — hover reveals subtle color shifts, no aggressive animations. Scrolling feels like leafing through a luxury magazine.

**Animation**:
- Scroll-triggered fade-ins with 0.6s ease
- Photo hover: slight scale(1.02) with brightness increase
- Button: border-color transition on hover

**Typography System**:
- Display: `Cormorant Garamond` (ultra-elegant serif)
- Body: `Source Serif 4` (readable serif for body)
- Labels: `Montserrat` (small caps, uppercase tracking)

## Idea 3: "Boutique Agency" — Dark Cinematic with Neon Rose

**Design Movement**: Dark boutique creative agency — premium, modern, with a feminine edge

**Core Principles**:
1. Cinematic darkness as canvas — near-black backgrounds make content pop
2. Rose/blush as the signature accent — matches her Instagram pink script aesthetic
3. Full-bleed immersive sections — no visible page edges, everything bleeds
4. Motion as storytelling — animations reinforce the narrative arc

**Color Philosophy**:
- Background: `#111111` (deep black)
- Rose: `#e8a0b0` (soft rose, matches Instagram pink)
- Gold: `#d4a853` (warm amber, cabin lights)
- Cream: `#f8f2ea` (text)
- Deep Green: `#1c2b1c` (card backgrounds)

**Layout Paradigm**: Full-screen sections with centered hero, then alternating asymmetric content blocks. Service packages displayed as premium "membership cards" with hover flip effect.

**Signature Elements**:
1. Thin rose-colored horizontal rules
2. Large background numbers (01, 02, 03) behind section titles
3. Glowing amber/rose gradient on CTA buttons

**Interaction Philosophy**: Every interaction feels premium — smooth, never jarring. Scroll-triggered reveals, magnetic hover on buttons.

**Animation**:
- Hero: video loop background with dark overlay
- Text: character-by-character reveal on hero title
- Cards: 3D tilt on hover
- Scroll: smooth momentum scrolling

**Typography System**:
- Display: `Fraunces` (optical serif, cinematic)
- Script: `Caveat` (handwritten feel for accents)
- Body: `DM Sans` (clean, modern)
